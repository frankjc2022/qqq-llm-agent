Published on: 2024-12-10 22:45:00+00:00
Alphabet (GOOG, GOOGL) stock jumps on news of Google's new quantum computing chip, Willow. The Futurum Group chief market strategist Cory Johnson joins Julie Hyman and Josh Lipton to discuss what the news means for Alphabet and the quantum computing space. The strategist explains that quantum computing "in theory, will be able to do incredible things... It could someday be able to do just incredible things that are hard to imagine here. And [the] science has to happen long before a product can happen. But there's no sense that any product is going to happen anytime soon."
