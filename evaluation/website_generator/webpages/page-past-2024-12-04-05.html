"When a company gets considered to be a monopoly by the federal government, that hasn't historically been a bad thing for their stock. If you look at companies, whenever the federal government started considering them to be a monopoly, those have been some pretty big winners in terms of a stock price thereafter," the strategist says, highlighting Microsoft's (MSFT) gains since its antitrust woes in the 1990s.
