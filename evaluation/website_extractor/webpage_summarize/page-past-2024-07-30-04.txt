The latest news highlights significant developments in the tech industry, particularly focusing on Tesla and Meta Platforms. 

Tesla is under scrutiny following a tragic incident in Seattle where a motorcyclist was killed by a Tesla vehicle operating on its "Full Self Driving" system. CEO Elon Musk has expressed confidence that the self-driving system will soon operate without human oversight. Additionally, Tesla has issued a recall for nearly 2 million vehicles due to a software flaw related to the hood latch, which has affected its stock performance.

He noted that while there will be a period of adjustment before the benefits of these investments are realized, the current climate is pivotal for tech firms.

Meta Platforms is also in the spotlight as it prepares for its Q2 earnings report amidst a busy earnings week for major tech companies. The company has reached a $1.4 billion settlement over facial recognition data issues in Texas, which raises questions about its regulatory challenges. Despite these hurdles, Meta is rolling out new AI tools in the EU, although it has faced previous regulatory setbacks.
