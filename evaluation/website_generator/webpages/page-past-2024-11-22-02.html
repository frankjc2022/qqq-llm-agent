"We really are at the building stage of AI," Epistrophy Capital Research chief market strategist Cory Johnson tells Morning Brief, calling it "a real life-changing thing." However, Johnson raises concerns about Nvidia's market position: "I'm really curious about what the partners of Nvidia are going to say to Nvidia three years from now. I mean [I] wonder if that's going to create bad feelings among customers."
