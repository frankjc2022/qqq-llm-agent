Meta reported better-than-expected earnings but cautioned about significant capital expenditures anticipated for 2025, indicating a strategic shift towards increased investment in technology. Meanwhile, Microsoft exceeded earnings estimates but provided cautious guidance for its cloud business, leading to a dip in its stock price.
